Our Story

Built for the Trades Industry

V&M Agency was founded with one mission: bring the power of AI and modern marketing to the businesses that keep communities running — contractors, plumbers, electricians, HVAC technicians, and every other trade that gets their hands dirty.

Most agencies don't understand your world. They pitch generic solutions that don't account for the way trades businesses actually operate — unpredictable schedules, high call volumes, leads that go cold fast, and owners who are too busy on job sites to manage a marketing campaign.

We solve that. Our AI systems work in the background while you focus on the work. Calls get answered. Leads get followed up. Appointments get booked. And you get to focus on what you do best.
